Overview

Site Name

Ruai Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the town in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Ruai Chamber of Commerce aims to facilitate networking among our members by providing opportunities to interact with potential partners, suppliers and customers. We support local business by providing market knowledge, guidance towards compliance with regulatory framework. Through our common voice, we lobby for the business interest of our members at both county and national level.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find information about the legal requirements for operating my kind of business or trade requirments for accessing foreign markets?
  • Where can I find information regarding the chamber's official stance on emerging or controversial tax issues?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
  • As a member, where can I find contact information of other member businesses within the network?
